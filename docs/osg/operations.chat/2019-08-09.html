(13:25:25) matyas: @paschos topology registration for stashcaches is used for generating their "authfiles"... getting an authfile for the cache from topology will let you verify that registration is correct. so try this:

$ curl 'https://topology.opensciencegrid.org/stashcache/authfile?cache_fqdn=stashcache.grid.uchicago.edu' | less

if you get a bunch of xrootd acls (stuff like g /hcc /hcc/focusday rl and u 47146b14.0 /user/ligo rl) then your registration is fine. if you get an error message, it's not
(13:27:12) matyas: that's for the authenticated stashcache; for the unauthenticated stashcache, it's 

$ curl 'https://topology.opensciencegrid.org/stashcache/authfile-public?cache_fqdn=stashcache.grid.uchicago.edu' | less

("/stashcache/authfile-public" instead of "/stashcache/authfile")
