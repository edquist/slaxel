(12:48:03) matyas: I believe there's a way to specify the pattern of files to skip; I think it's in /etc/condor-ce/condor_config
(12:52:50) matyas: although that file gets overwritten on upgrades so you can't really change it.
LOCAL_CONFIG_DIR_EXCLUDE_REGEXP = ^((\..*)|(.*~)|(#.*)|(.*\.rpmsave)|(.*\.rpmnew))$

skips files starting with . (hidden) or # (emacs temp file); skips files ending with ~ (backup) or .rpmsave or .rpmnew (backup created by rpm)
