(20:25:45) dwd: *please* do not do it with a large number, say more than 10 or so, jobs because shub is not designed to serve large numbers. It does not have the caching of cvmfs, nor the efficiency of only downloading the pieces that it accesses. In addition, we are moving the grid toward unprivileged singularity, and it will only be able to work with unpacked directories like in cvmfs because mounting a loopback device is a privileged operation. The OSG singularity install instructions also encourages people to disable loopback devices even with privileged singularity, so you may find that it does not work at some sites.
